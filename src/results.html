Your score is

Financial well being: (could be improved, average, high)

Scores range up to 95, but there’s no specific cut off for a “good” or a “bad” score. Instead, your score might give you ideas about small steps you can take to build your finances to where you want to be.

this is where the graph will go


Even if your score isn’t where you want it to be, it’s important to remember that you’re still in control. There are small steps you can choose to take which can lead to big changes. Whether you’re looking for more control over your day-to-day finances, or meeting longer-term financial goals, or something in between, we have tools to help.
